nixpkgs / mate.mate-netbook - MATE utilities for netbooks are an applet and a daemon to maximize windows and move their titles on the panel. Installing these utilities is recommended for netbooks and similar devices with low resolution displays.

Homepage - https://mate-desktop.org

Maintainers - Johannes Lötzsch - José Romildo Malaquias
