nixpkgs / nuclei - Nuclei is used to send requests across targets based on a template leading to zero false positives and providing effective scanning for known paths. Main use cases for nuclei are during initial reconnaissance phase to quickly check for low hanging fruits or CVEs across targets that are known and easily detectable.

Homepage - https://github.com/projectdiscovery/nuclei
