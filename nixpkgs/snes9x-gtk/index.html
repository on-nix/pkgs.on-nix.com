nixpkgs / snes9x-gtk - Snes9x is a portable, freeware Super Nintendo Entertainment System (SNES) emulator. It basically allows you to play most games designed for the SNES and Super Famicom Nintendo game systems on your PC or Workstation; which includes some real gems that were only ever released in Japan.

Homepage - https://www.snes9x.com

Maintainers - Joachim Schiele
