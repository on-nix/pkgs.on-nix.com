nixpkgs / insync - Insync is a commercial application that syncs your Drive files to your computer. It has more advanced features than Google's official client such as multiple account support, Google Doc conversion, symlink support, and built in sharing. There is a 15-day free trial, and it is a paid application after that.

Homepage - https://www.insynchq.com

Maintainers - Benjamin Staffin
