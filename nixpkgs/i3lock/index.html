nixpkgs / i3lock - Simple screen locker. After locking, a colored background (default: white) or a configurable image is shown, and a ring-shaped unlock-indicator gives feedback for every keystroke. After entering your password, the screen is unlocked again.

Homepage - https://i3wm.org/i3lock/
