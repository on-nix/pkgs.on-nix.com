nixpkgs / profile-cleaner - Use profile-cleaner to reduce the size of browser profiles by organizing their sqlite databases using sqlite3's vacuum and reindex functions. The term "browser" is used loosely since profile-cleaner happily works on some email clients and newsreaders too.

Homepage - https://github.com/graysky2/profile-cleaner
