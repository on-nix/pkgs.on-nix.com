nixpkgs / micronaut - Micronaut is a modern, JVM-based, full stack microservices framework designed for building modular, easily testable microservice applications. Reflection-based IoC frameworks load and cache reflection data for every single field, method, and constructor in your code, whereas with Micronaut, your application startup time and memory consumption are not bound to the size of your codebase.

Homepage - https://micronaut.io/
