nixpkgs / rr - rr aspires to be your primary debugging tool, replacing -- well, enhancing -- gdb. You record a failure once, then debug the recording, deterministically, as many times as you want. Every time the same execution is replayed.

Homepage - https://rr-project.org/

Maintainers - Nicolas B. Pierron - Austin Seipp
