nixpkgs / gxemul - GXemul is a framework for full-system computer architecture emulation. Several real machines have been implemented within the framework, consisting of processors (ARM, MIPS, Motorola 88K, PowerPC, and SuperH) and surrounding hardware components such as framebuffers, interrupt controllers, busses, disk controllers, and serial controllers. The emulation is working well enough to allow several unmodified "guest" operating systems to run.

Homepage - http://gavare.se/gxemul/
