nixpkgs / logcheck - Mails anomalies in the system logfiles to the administrator. Logcheck helps spot problems and security violations in your logfiles automatically and will send the results to you by e-mail. Logcheck was part of the Abacus Project of security tools, but this version has been rewritten.

Homepage - https://salsa.debian.org/debian/logcheck
