nixpkgs / ammonite - The Ammonite-REPL is an improved Scala REPL, re-implemented from first principles. It is much more featureful than the default REPL and comes with a lot of ergonomic improvements and configurability that may be familiar to people coming from IDEs or other REPLs such as IPython or Zsh.

Homepage - https://www.lihaoyi.com/Ammonite/

License - MIT

Maintainers - Tim Steinbach
