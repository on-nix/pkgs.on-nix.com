nixpkgs / ocamlPackages.menhir - Menhir is a LR(1) parser generator for the Objective Caml programming language. That is, Menhir compiles LR(1) grammar specifications down to OCaml code. Menhir was designed and implemented by François Pottier and Yann Régis-Gianas.

Homepage - http://pauillac.inria.fr/~fpottier/menhir/

Maintainers - Vincent Laporte
