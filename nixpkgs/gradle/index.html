nixpkgs / gradle - Gradle is a build system which offers you ease, power and freedom. You can choose the balance for yourself. It has powerful multi-project build support. It has a layer on top of Ivy that provides a build-by-convention integration for Ivy. It gives you always the choice between the flexibility of Ant and the convenience of a build-by-convention behavior.
