nixpkgs / kodiPackages.svtplay - With this addon you can stream content from SVT Play (svtplay.se). The plugin fetches the video URL from the SVT Play website and feeds it to the Kodi video player. HLS (m3u8) is the preferred video format by the plugin.

Homepage - https://forum.kodi.tv/showthread.php?tid=67110

License - GPL-3.0-or-later

Maintainers - Aaron Andersen - Carles Pagès - Edward Tjörnhammar - Rémi Nicole - Peter Hoeg - Stefan Wiehler
