nixpkgs / coqPackages.hydra-battles - An exploration of some properties of Kirby and Paris' hydra battles, with the help of the Coq Proof assistant. This development includes the study of several representations of ordinal numbers, and a part of the so-called Ketonen and Solovay machinery (combinatorial properties of epsilon0).

Homepage - https://github.com/coq-community/hydra-battles
