nixpkgs / python39Packages.grappelli_safe - grappelli_safe was created to provide a snapshot of the Grappelli admin skin for Django, to be referenced as a dependency for the Mezzanine CMS for Django. At the time of grappelli_safe's creation, Grappelli was incorrectly packaged on PyPI, and had also dropped compatibility with Django 1.1 - grappelli_safe was therefore created to address these specific issues.

Homepage - https://github.com/stephenmcd/grappelli-safe

Maintainers - Pavan Rikhi
