nixpkgs / radeontop - View GPU utilization, both for the total activity percent and individual blocks. Supports R600 and later cards: even Southern Islands should work. Works with both the open drivers and AMD Catalyst. Total GPU utilization is also valid for OpenCL loads; the other blocks are only useful for GL loads. Requires root rights or other permissions to read /dev/mem.

Homepage - https://github.com/clbr/radeontop
