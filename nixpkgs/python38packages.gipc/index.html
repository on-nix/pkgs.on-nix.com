nixpkgs / python38Packages.gipc - Usage of Python's multiprocessing package in a gevent-powered application may raise problems and most likely breaks the application in various subtle ways. gipc (pronunciation "gipsy") is developed with the motivation to solve many of these issues transparently. With gipc, multiprocessing. Process-based child processes can safely be created anywhere within your gevent-powered application.

Homepage - http://gehrcke.de/gipc
