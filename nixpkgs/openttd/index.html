nixpkgs / openttd - OpenTTD is a transportation economics simulator. In single player mode, players control a transportation business, and use rail, road, sea, and air transport to move goods and people around the simulated world. In multiplayer networked mode, players may: - play competitively as different businesses - play cooperatively controlling the same business - observe as spectators

Homepage - https://www.openttd.org/
