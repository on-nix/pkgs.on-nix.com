nixpkgs / tor - Tor helps improve your privacy by bouncing your communications around a network of relays run by volunteers all around the world: it makes it harder for somebody watching your Internet connection to learn what sites you visit, and makes it harder for the sites you visit to track you. Tor works with many of your existing applications, including web browsers, instant messaging clients, remote login, and other applications based on the TCP protocol.
