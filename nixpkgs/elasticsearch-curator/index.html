nixpkgs / elasticsearch-curator - Elasticsearch Curator helps you curate, or manage, your Elasticsearch indices and snapshots by: * Obtaining the full list of indices (or snapshots) from the cluster, as the actionable list * Iterate through a list of user-defined filters to progressively remove indices (or snapshots) from this actionable list as needed. * Perform various actions on the items which remain in the actionable list.

Homepage - https://github.com/elastic/curator

License - Apache-2.0

Maintainers - Bas van Dijk
