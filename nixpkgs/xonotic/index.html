nixpkgs / xonotic - Xonotic is a free, fast-paced first-person shooter that works on Windows, macOS and Linux. The project is geared towards providing addictive arena shooter gameplay which is all spawned and driven by the community itself. Xonotic is a direct successor of the Nexuiz project with years of development between them, and it aims to become the best possible open-source FPS of its kind.
