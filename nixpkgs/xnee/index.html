nixpkgs / xnee - Xnee is a suite of programs that can record, replay and distribute user actions under the X11 environment. Think of it as a robot that can imitate the job you just did. Xnee can be used to automate tests, demonstrate programs, distribute actions, record & replay "macros", retype a file.

Homepage - https://www.gnu.org/software/xnee/
