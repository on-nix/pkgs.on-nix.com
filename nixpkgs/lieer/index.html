nixpkgs / lieer - This program can pull email and labels (and changes to labels) from your GMail account and store them locally in a maildir with the labels synchronized with a notmuch database. The changes to tags in the notmuch database may be pushed back remotely to your GMail account.

Homepage - https://lieer.gaute.vetsj.com/
