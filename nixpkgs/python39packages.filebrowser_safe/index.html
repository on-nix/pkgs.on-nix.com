nixpkgs / python39Packages.filebrowser_safe - filebrowser_safe was created to provide a snapshot of the FileBrowser asset manager for Django, to be referenced as a dependency for the Mezzanine CMS for Django. At the time of filebrowser_safe's creation, FileBrowser was incorrectly packaged on PyPI, and had also dropped compatibility with Django 1.1 - filebrowser_safe was therefore created to address these specific issues.

Homepage - https://github.com/stephenmcd/filebrowser-safe

Maintainers - Pavan Rikhi
