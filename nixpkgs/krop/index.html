nixpkgs / krop - Krop is a tool that allows you to optimise your PDF files, and remove sections of the page you do not want. A unique feature of krop, at least to my knowledge, is its ability to automatically split pages into subpages to fit the limited screensize of devices such as eReaders. This is particularly useful, if your eReader does not support convenient scrolling. Krop also has a command line interface.

Homepage - http://arminstraub.com/software/krop
