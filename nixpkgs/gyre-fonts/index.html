nixpkgs / gyre-fonts - The Gyre project started in 2006, and will eventually include enhanced releases of all 35 freely available PostScript fonts distributed with Ghostscript v4.00. These are being converted to OpenType and extended with diacritical marks covering all modern European languages and then some

Homepage - http://www.gust.org.pl/projects/e-foundry/tex-gyre/index_html#Readings

License - LPPL-1.3c

Maintainers - Daniel Bergey
