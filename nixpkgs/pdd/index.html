nixpkgs / pdd - There are times you want to check how old you are (in years, months, days) or how long you need to wait for the next flash sale or the number of days left of your notice period in your current job. pdd (Python3 Date Diff) is a small cmdline utility to calculate date and time difference. If no program arguments are specified it shows the current date, time and timezone.
