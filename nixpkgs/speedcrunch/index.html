nixpkgs / speedcrunch - SpeedCrunch is a fast, high precision and powerful desktop calculator. Among its distinctive features are a scrollable display, up to 50 decimal precisions, unlimited variable storage, intelligent automatic completion full keyboard-friendly and more than 15 built-in math function.
