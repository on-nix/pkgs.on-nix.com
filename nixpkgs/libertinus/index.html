nixpkgs / libertinus - The Libertinus font project began as a fork of the Linux Libertine and Linux Biolinum fonts. The original impetus was to add an OpenType math companion to the Libertine font families. Over time it grew into to a full-fledged fork addressing many of the bugs in the Libertine fonts.

Homepage - https://github.com/alerque/libertinus
