nixpkgs / weston - Weston is the reference implementation of a Wayland compositor, as well as a useful environment in and of itself. Out of the box, Weston provides a very basic desktop, or a full-featured environment for non-desktop uses such as automotive, embedded, in-flight, industrial, kiosks, set-top boxes and TVs. It also provides a library allowing other projects to build their own full-featured environments on top of Weston's core. A small suite of example or demo clients are also provided.

Homepage - https://gitlab.freedesktop.org/wayland/weston
