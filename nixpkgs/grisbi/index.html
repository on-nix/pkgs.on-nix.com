nixpkgs / grisbi - Grisbi is an application written by French developers, so it perfectly respects French accounting rules. Grisbi can manage multiple accounts, currencies and users. It manages third party, expenditure and receipt categories, budgetary lines, financial years, budget estimates, bankcard management and other information that make Grisbi adapted for associations.

Homepage - https://grisbi.org
